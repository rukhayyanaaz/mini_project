Environmental Sanitation


  • Environmental Sanitation includes human excreta control, managing solid waste and waste water.
  • Sanitation and environmental hygiene issues must be analyzed during the initial assessment.
  • In CE's considerations for sanitation needs are included during the camp planning and layout..
  • Good personal hygiene helps stop you from getting sick and spreading germs.
  • Washing your hands often is a good way to stop the germs on your hands making you sick.
  • Bathing or showering often helps prevent infection and body lice.
